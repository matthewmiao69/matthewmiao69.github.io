The dream

3 minute read, Poem
Written by Tony Lang

I have seen the world

I have dived into the pits of hell
turning and weaving whilst those doomed
smile and wave along
desperately trying to pull me down.
But without fail, I rise every time.


I have lived in the abyss.
Countless suns soaring in the sky
with an eagle’s dumb pride
try to light up Tartarus.
Yet despite my self imposed doom,
without fail I rise every time.


I have run along the fields of Nirvana.
Fate’s will has given me strength
far unreachable to my pathetic soul.
The pack gallops forward-
a group far too good to be my own.
My head sinks down,
but without fail, I rise every time.


And yet despite the highs and lows,
the countless journeys,
the agonising smiles and
the tears lost to my soul,
I have not seen the world.


I had a dream not long ago.
